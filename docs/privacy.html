Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

The short version: Meera collects only what it needs to function. We store your Discord user ID and the data you explicitly create (notes, submissions, XP). We do not sell, share, or monetize your data. Ever.

1. Information We Collect

Meera collects the following data when you interact with the bot:

Data Purpose Stored
Discord User ID Identify your account across sessions Yes
XP and Streak Data Gamification, progress tracking Yes
Code Submissions Track solved problems Yes
Notes Your personal second brain Yes
AI Questions Generate responses Not stored by Meera
Discord Username Display on leaderboard Fetched live, not stored

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Meera does not collect, access, or store:

  • Your email address or password
  • Private or direct messages
  • Messages in channels where Meera is not mentioned or invoked
  • Voice channel data or audio
  • Server messages or chat history
  • Personal information beyond your Discord user ID

3. AI and Third-Party Services

When you use the "Ask Meera" feature, your question is sent to a third-party AI provider (currently Groq) to generate a response.

Important: Meera does not store your AI conversations. However, the third-party AI provider may process and temporarily retain your input in accordance with their own privacy policy. We recommend not sharing sensitive personal information through the AI feature.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on secure cloud infrastructure
  • Database access is restricted and not publicly accessible
  • We use encrypted connections for all database communications
  • No data is shared with third parties for advertising or analytics

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as you actively use Meera. If you wish to have your data deleted, you may request deletion at any time (see Section 7). Upon request, all data associated with your Discord user ID will be permanently removed from our database within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

Meera is not intended for users under the age of 13, in compliance with Discord's Terms of Service and COPPA regulations. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we become aware that a user is under 13, their data will be promptly deleted.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of all data we store about you
  • Delete — Request permanent deletion of all your data
  • Rectify — Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Withdraw — Stop using Meera at any time by simply not interacting with the bot

To exercise any of these rights, contact us through the channels listed in Section 9.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of Meera after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, data requests, or concerns:

  • GitHub: github.com/xau-aman/Meera-Bot
  • Discord: Open an issue or contact the developer directly
